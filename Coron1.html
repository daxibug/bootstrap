Beaches & Islands

Malcapuya Island

Located off the southern coast of Coron town in Palawan Island, Malcapuya Island is often hailed as one of the most beautiful beaches in the region. With its long stretch of powdery white sand, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and swaying palm trees, Malcapuya Island offers a postcard-perfect tropical escape. The beach is calm and pristine, making it ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and snorkeling—far from the crowds of more commercialized shores.

This island gem is part of the tour to Coron's best beaches or what is also known as island hopping Tour C, which also includes stops at Banana and Bulog Dos Islands. Joining this island hopping experience is one of the best ways to witness the raw coastal beauty that makes Coron town one of the top beach destinations in the Philippines.

Activities to do

  • Snorkeling & Swimming
  • Beach Relaxation & Sunbathing
  • Island Hopping Tours
  • Photography
  • Beach Activities
  • Picnicking & Dining
  • Stargazing

CYC Beach

CYC Beach, short for Coron Youth Club Beach, is one of the most accessible and family-friendly beaches in Coron Town, Palawan Island. Unlike most of Coron’s top island hopping destinations that require entrance fees (often included in tour packages), CYC Beach is free to enter and offers a laid-back vibe, perfect for travelers looking to relax, swim, and snorkel without the crowds.

Fringed by white sand, crystal-clear waters, and lush mangroves, the beach features a gently sloping shoreline that makes it ideal for kids and casual swimmers. Its shallow reef areas also make it a great stop for beginner snorkelers. Because it’s less commercialized than other top Coron town attractions, CYC Beach provides a more rustic, peaceful experience amidst stunning island views.

Activities to do

  • Snorkeling & Swimming
  • Relaxing & Sunbathing
  • Island Hopping Stop
  • Photography

Banana Island

Banana Island, often overlooked by the average tourist, is a hidden gem tucked away in the luscious province of Palawan in the Philippines. Known to locals as Dicalabuan Island, this slice of paradise offers pristine white sandy beaches surrounded by crystal-clear turquoise waters, making it an idyllic retreat away from the bustling tourist spots. With its curved shape reminiscent of a banana, it is a perfect sanctuary for those seeking peace and natural beauty.

Activities to do

  • Snorkeling & Swmming
  • Relaxing in Hammocks
  • Island Hopping & Beach Hopping
  • Beach Picnics & Dining
  • Walking & Photography
  • Volleyball

Banol Beach

Banol Beach is situated in Coron, Palawan. The waters are brilliantly blue, the beaches are perfectly white, and the sheer cliffs will greet you with open views. Go sunbathing on the gorgeous white beaches. Aside from swimming in the nearby reef garden, you can stretch your nature photography muscles.

Activities to do

  • Picnicking & Lunch
  • Swmming & Snorkeling
  • Relaxing & Sunbathing
  • Kayaking
  • Photography
