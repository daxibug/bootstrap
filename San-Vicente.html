Beaches

Long Beach

The longest white sand beach in the Philippines, stretching approximately 14.7 kilometers. Located in San Vicente, it is known for its cream-colored sand, shallow turquoise waters, and as an emerging sustainable tourism destination that is roughly three times longer than Boracay’s White sand beach.

Activities to do

  • Island Hopping to Boayan Island
  • Visit Port Barton
  • Snorkeling & Swimming
  • Explore Nearby Islands
  • Visit "Bato ni Ning Ning"

Maxima Beach

Maxima Island lies off the coast of Port Barton beach village in San Vicente town, Palawan, and is one of the less trodden jewels in the province’s archipelago of islets. Locals refer to it as Maxima, though some maps list it as Albaguen Island, and it is often included in the municipal island-hopping tours meant to showcase San Vicente town’s natural appeal.

Activities to do

  • Giant Trampoline Slide
  • Water Blob
  • Snorkeling & Diving
  • Banana Boat & Jetski Rides
  • Kayaking
  • Cliff Diving/Jumping

Alimanguan Beach

Palawan, an archipelagic province of the Philippines, is home to the hidden gem known as Alimanguan Beach. Tucked away on the stunning coastline, this beach is a tranquil paradise characterized by pristine sands that stretch out into the distance and clear waters that effortlessly blend with the horizon. It's a place that isn't overwhelmed by commercial development, allowing nature's beauty to take center stage.

Activities to do

  • Surfing
  • Sunset Viewing
  • Beachcombing & Relaxation

Capsalay Island

Capsalay Island is more than just an island in Port Barton, Palawan. Some have described it as “paradise”, for its beauty, its remoteness and the relaxing atmosphear that reigns on the islet… This is a great place to observe the daily life of fishermen, take a walk on the beach or enjoy a fresh buko juice. You can visit thanks to tours offered by local organizers on Tikigo. Just check them out below and start planning your adventures online today.

Activities to do

  • Island Hopping & Snorkeling
  • Beacg Relaxation & walking
  • Glamping at Capsalay Reef Camp
  • Cultural Observation

Caruray Beach

Caruray Beach, located in San Vicente, Palawan, is a serene, untouched destination known for its raw natural beauty, featuring lush greenery, expansive coastlines, and stunning morning views. It offers a quiet escape often visited by motorbike from Port Barton, ideal for nature lovers seeking a tranquil alternative to popular tourist spots.

Activities to do

  • Beachcombing
  • Swimming & Snorkeling
  • Motorbike Day Trips
  • Nature Sightseeing
  • Picnicking

Coconut Beach

Coconut Beach is one of the most picturesque spots in Port Barton, boasting a pristine shoreline fringed with swaying coconut palms and crystal-clear waters. It’s a tropical paradise perfect for relaxing and soaking in the beauty of nature.As the name suggests, Coconut Beach has one of the most stunning coconut-lined shorelines in Palawan.

Activities to do

  • Beach Games
  • Swimming & Snorkeling
  • Sea Kayaking
  • Hiking/Trekking
  • Relaxation
  • Dining & Nightlife

Paradise Island

Paradise Island, located in Port Barton, San Vicente, Palawan, is a charming stopover on a boat tour. The island offers a delightful beach and the opportunity to savor a lunch cooked over an open flame. Visitors can also purchase affordable beer and enjoy the cool ambiance. This little slice of paradise features picturesque views, including a rock formation perfect for Instagram-worthy photos and excellent snorkeling spots where you can encounter vibrant coral reefs and meet 'Nemo' clownfish.

Activities to do

  • Relaxation & Sunbathing
  • Swimming & Snorkeling
  • Photography
  • Enjoy Island Refreshments
  • Picnic

Inaladelan Island

With its crystal-clear waters, creamy white sand beach, and an excellent snorkeling spot, Inaladelan Island is easily one of my favorite islands in Palawan. It’s a popular lunch stop for island-hopping tours. Here you can relax under nipa hut-style umbrellas while sipping on cocktails or fresh coconut juice, take a refreshing dip in the ocean, or unwind with an afternoon nap in the cozy hammocks scattered along the beach.

Activities to do

  • Beach Sports
  • Swimming & Snorkeling
  • Relaxation & Beaches
  • Overnight Camping
  • Turtle Conservation
  • Dining & Socializing
  • Island Hopping Stop
